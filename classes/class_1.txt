Restaurant class: Meals

Attributes: 
1. Ingredients (Array)
2. Appetizer (Boolean)
3. Name (String)

Methods:
1. Sautee (This method can change the ingredient attribute ex. onions to satueed onions)
2. Bake (This method can also change the ingredient attribute ex. chicken to baked chicken)
3. Add_cheese (This method can change the name attribute ex. hamburger to cheeseburger and the ingredients attribute ex. baked potato to baked potato with cheese.)
